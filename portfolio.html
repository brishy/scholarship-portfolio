My journey




5/2014

  • Developed a strong command of the English language through independent learning in 2007.
  • Secured a bronze medal at a countrywide level in the International Olympiads of English (IOE) competition in 2014, at the age of nine, competing against participants one to two years older.
  • Overcame obstacles such as pressure to perform, balancing academics with passion, and overcoming self-doubt during the IOE competition.
  • Turned pressure into motivation, pushed self to work harder and improve English language skills, allowing for confident competition.
  • Gained valuable lessons in perseverance, dedication, and hard work, as well as stepping out of comfort zones and taking risks.

4/2017 - 2020


  • Began teaching English and coding for free as part of charity activities at Everest English Center in April 2017 for underprivileged kids.
  • Committed to instilling a sense of pride and accomplishment in students, helping them unlock their full potential and opening doors to new opportunities.



5/2018 - Present

  • Co-founded an English club at Cao Thanh Primary School in Ha Long in May 2018 to improve young students' language skills.
  • Developed an interactive curriculum incorporating innovative teaching techniques and enjoyable activities to help students gain confidence in their English-speaking abilities and develop essential teamwork and communication skills.
  • Coordinated meetings and managed resources, mentored students, and guided volunteer team as the co-founder and leader of the club.
  • Honed organizational and leadership skills, as well as strengthened resolve to empower the youth in the community.





7/2019 - 7/2022

  • Graduated High School for Gifted Students (HSGS) at Hanoi University of Science with a 3.81 GPA (9.4/10) (Top 5 national educational institution to nurture talented Vietnamese students who excelled at natural sciences)
  • Achieved success in an environment that fostered communication, exploration, and creativity.
  • Advanced mathematics curriculum provided a perfect environment to push boundaries and explore potential.
  • Developed crucial leadership skills by relying on each other's strengths and supporting one another through academic journey.

10/2020

  • Achieved an overall score of 7.0 on the IELTS exam in October 2020
  • Offered IELTS preparation and coaching to assist others in need.
  • Developed a personalized, one-on-one coaching method to deliver a deeper grasp of the exam's complexities and success techniques.
  • Gained vital lessons about tenacity, patience, and the necessity of continuous self-improvement while assisting others.
  • Retook the IELTS exam in December 2022, achieving an overall score of 8.0 with a reading score of 9.
  • Demonstrated progress as both a learner and a coach, and the capacity to adapt and enhance teaching tactics, lead students to achieve their IELTS goals.











8/2022 - Present

  • Joined #100devs agency training and contract work program
  • Improved coding skills and gained valuable work, enhancing portfolio and professional skills
  • Collaborated with like-minded individuals through building scalable projects such as Filmpire, Tidal Music Player, The Tech Times , motivating others to push limits and grow as developers
  • Earned certificates from various online platforms such as Mimo, Gymnasium ,freeCodeCamp ,CodeCademy, Meta and Coursera , showcasing proficiency and commitment to learning
  • Discovered passion for problem-solving, collaboration, and creating innovative solutions with long-lasting positive impacts
  • Embraced lifelong learning and persistence, with support from #100devs community and personal drive to excel
